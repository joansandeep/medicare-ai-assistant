Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request medications?
Go to the Medicine section from your dashboard, fill in the medicine name and quantity, then submit your request. You'll receive updates via email and in your activities.
How can I access my medical reports?
All your medical reports are available in the Reports section. You can view, download, and share them with your healthcare providers as needed.
Is my medical data secure?
Yes, we use bank-level encryption and follow strict HIPAA compliance standards. Your data is stored securely and is never shared without your explicit consent.
How long does medicine processing take?
Common medicines are processed within 2-4 hours, specialized medicines take 1-2 days, and emergency requests are handled within 30 minutes.
